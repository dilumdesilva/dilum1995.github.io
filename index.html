Bit More About Dilum

Dilum De Silva is a passionate technologist, dedicated mentor, and proud father to his son, Denver. Outside of engineering, he enjoys road trips in his beloved Pajero, often spending his holidays upgrading and refining it.

Professionally, Dilum is driven by delivering meaningful value through technology while uplifting those around him to achieve strong team performance. With over 8 years of experience building mobile solutions, he has contributed across diverse industries including Farming Management, Financial Management, the Digital Telco sector, Ride Hailing platforms, Fruit Produce supply systems, and Research & Development, while also working on cutting-edge AI integrations for mobile platforms.

Alongside his industry work, Dilum serves as a visiting lecturer at the University of Westminster affiliated IIT Sri Lanka, where he supervises bachelor's level research projects and supports the next generation of engineers. He currently works as a Senior Software Engineer at TopRail Limited.
